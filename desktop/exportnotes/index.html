Can I Export Mockup Notes?

home > desktop > exportnotes

When you export your mockups to PDF you can choose to export the notes that you’ve written in the notes panel by checking the box labeled “Include mockup notes.”

Exported images do not include mockup notes.

Another way to share mockup notes is to share the project file.

If you are using myBalsamiq you can invite people to join your project so that they can see the notes and comments you’ve made and add their own.
